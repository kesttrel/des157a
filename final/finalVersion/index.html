Rules

  1. There are 2 players.
  2. The player whose turn it is rolls the dice
  3. The total of the roll is added to the current players score and the player rolls again
  4. If a 1 is rolled, the turn is passed to the next player
  5. After each roll the player can either roll again, or if they feel their luck is running thin, they can pass their turn to the next player
  6. If either player rolls a double 1 or “snake eyes” their total score is reset to 0
